WHAT INFORMATION IS COLLECTED AND HOW DO WE USE IT?

TYPES OF INFORMATION COLLECTED

We will ask you for personal information that is necessary for us to successfully provide you with our services. If you do not provide us with this personal information, we may not be able to provide our products or services to you.

We may collect the following types of information (which may or may not be personal information):

Device-related data: Data related to your device's hardware, performance, status, etc., such as OS version, manufacturer information, model, device and motherboard identification number, MAC address, the name and status (online/offline) of your device.

Network-related data: This includes the WIFI SSID of the router, RSSI, IP address, and BSSID.

Information specific to you that may be assigned by us: We may collect and use information such as your Mi Account ID.

Videos recorded due to detected movement: When the Home Surveillance Assistant or Mi Cloud is enabled, the camera will start video recording if movement is detected (Home Surveillance Assistant will save the first 10 seconds of the video; if Mi Cloud is enabled, the whole video, from the time when movement is detected to the time when movement ends, will be saved). The video will be uploaded to a server. You can view or manage it via the app. The video will only be kept on the server for 7 days, after which it will be automatically deleted.

Videos on the memory card: If a memory card is inserted in your camera, information about the videos recorded by the camera may be stored on the card. You can access the data directly from the memory card, or manage the videos on the memory card via the app. When the storage space on the memory card is low, the older videos will be overwritten by the new ones.

Real-time videos: Video streams are encrypted when users watch real-time videos on their mobile phones. Such encrypted video streams are transmitted through the server, but will not be saved on the server.
